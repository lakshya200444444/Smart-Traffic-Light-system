\chapter{Methodology}

The methodology of this project involves several key steps to develop an effective AI-based traffic management system:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Data Collection}\\
    Real-time traffic data will be collected using sensors, cameras, and GPS devices installed at various points in the traffic network. This data includes vehicle count, speed, congestion levels, and signal status.

    \item \textbf{Data Preprocessing}\\
    The collected raw data will be cleaned and organized to remove noise or errors. This step ensures that the AI algorithms receive accurate and relevant information for analysis.

    \item \textbf{Traffic Analysis Using AI}\\
    Machine learning models will be developed to analyze the traffic patterns based on the preprocessed data. These models will learn to detect congestion, predict traffic flow, and identify peak hours.

    \item \textbf{Intelligent Signal Control}\\
    The system will use the AI predictions to adjust traffic signal timings dynamically. This helps in reducing wait times and improving the overall flow of vehicles.

    \item \textbf{Emergency Vehicle Prioritization}\\
    The system will be designed to detect emergency vehicles and give them priority by adjusting signals accordingly to clear their path quickly.

    \item \textbf{System Integration and Testing}\\
    The components will be integrated into a working prototype. The system will be tested using simulated traffic scenarios to evaluate its performance and make improvements.

    \item \textbf{Deployment and Monitoring}\\
    After successful testing, the system can be deployed in a real traffic environment for continuous monitoring and further optimization based on feedback.
\end{enumerate}
